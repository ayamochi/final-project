Not a lot of people know that the gender pay gap still exists. To some, it may seem like something that used to be a problem. Because of this, our goal is to spark conversations between teens about this issue. In this website, we decided to provide some resources to hopefully inspire you to have a conversation with your peers about the gender gap now and the possible actions to take.
